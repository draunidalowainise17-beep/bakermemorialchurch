Welcome

A Lighthouse of
Faith in the Pacific

Baker Memorial Church stands as a testament to the enduring Christian faith in Fiji. Named in honour of the Reverend Thomas Baker, our church has long been a spiritual home for the community of Davuilevu and beyond.


As part of the Methodist Church in Fiji, we are committed to worship, discipleship, fellowship, and service — welcoming all who seek to know God and grow in His love. Whether you are a longtime member or visiting for the first time, you are warmly welcome here.

Our Community

The Davuilevu Community

Baker Memorial Church sits at the heart of a unique community shaped by three great Methodist institutions — each one devoted to forming lives of faith, knowledge, and practical service for Fiji and the Pacific.

Davuilevu Theological College

Training pastors and church leaders to serve the Methodist Church in Fiji, Rotuma, and across the Pacific with theological depth and pastoral care.

Methodist Lay Training College

Equipping laypeople with the skills and spiritual formation to serve their congregations and communities with confidence and commitment.

Lelean Memorial School

A proud Methodist secondary school nurturing the next generation of Fijian leaders through academic excellence, character, and Christian values.
